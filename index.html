Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about Full-Stack Development, Technical SEO, and High-Performance Web Solutions.

Which technology stack do you specialize in for building new applications?

I specialize in the modern **MERN stack** (**MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js**) for building fast, scalable, and dynamic web applications. I also utilize **Next.js** for server-side rendering, which is crucial for achieving superior SEO and lightning-fast loading speeds. My approach ensures your application is both technically robust and optimized for user experience (UX).

How do you guarantee that a website will rank higher in search engines?

I focus on **Technical SEO**, which involves optimizing the structural and architectural elements of your site. This includes improving **Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS)**, fixing broken internal links, optimizing render-blocking resources, and ensuring proper schema markup. By fixing these foundation issues, I eliminate the technical barriers preventing Google from ranking your site higher. My previous work has resulted in clients seeing **40-90% increases in organic traffic**.

Can you enhance my existing WordPress site beyond standard themes and plugins?

Absolutely. I go beyond basic customization. I provide **Advanced WordPress Development**, including custom **PHP coding**, developing bespoke plugins, building specialized themes, and integrating complex features like custom APIs and WooCommerce configurations. This ensures your WordPress site is secure, efficient, and capable of handling high traffic and complex functionality.

Do you build mobile applications and custom interactive tools?

Yes. I develop cross-platform mobile apps using **React Native**, allowing your application to run seamlessly on both iOS and Android from a single codebase. For highly specialized needs, such as educational training or simulations (like the MRI simulator I built), I use **Unity (C#)** to create sophisticated 3D interactive tools.

What is your process for quality assurance and project testing?

Quality and stability are paramount. My process includes rigorous testing of APIs and database integrity using tools like **Postman** to ensure all backend services function reliably. Frontend testing covers responsive design across all devices. We use **Git/GitHub** for version control and adhere to strict Agile methodologies to deliver clean, bug-free, and scalable code.

I need help with both development and SEO. How do we start?

The best way to begin is by filling out the form on my contact page. I'll need details about your project scope, target audience, and current site performance (if applicable). We will then schedule a free 30-minute consultation to define the objectives, discuss the ideal tech stack, and prepare a tailored proposal covering both development and long-term SEO strategy.
